Participants

The players at the n00bcon championship hails from 17 nationalities and about 40 different old school communities! From the far reaches of Europe across to the other side of the Atlantic ocean, mages gather to determine who's the Master of Magic Cards.

This page is a living document under construction, as many community organizors haven't given out their invites yet. If you are looking for an invite, grab a hold of your closest tournament organizor and ask him about the plans for n00bcon X. The n00bcon championship is unfortunately invite only.
