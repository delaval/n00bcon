Participants

From the far reaches of the old continent across to the other side of the Atlantic ocean, cunning mages gather to determine who's the true Master of Magic Cards. The players at the n00bcon championship represent a vast number of metas from over 40 different old school communities.

The Sharks are previous champions. Any Shark-winner may freely give away their invite to any other player they see fit if they wont make it themselves. This year we have also ear-marked a few spots for some of the most beloved content creators of the format. If any of these content creators are unable to make it, we will open those spots for other players to claim. The content creators with "(?)" after their name are currently unconfirmed.

This page is a living document under construction, as many community organizors haven't given out their invites yet. If you are looking for an invite, grab a hold of your closest tournament organizor and ask him or her about the plans for n00bcon 11. The n00bcon championship is unfortunately invite only.
